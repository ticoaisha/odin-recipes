Greek Salad

Classic refreshing Greek salad

Filled with fresh veggies, tangy feta, and a simple dressing made of ingredients you might already have in your fridge, this bright salad is one youâ€™ll turn to all spring and summer long.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced into half-moons
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 pt. grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 c. halved Kalamata olives /li>
  • 6 oz. feta, cut into 1/2" cubes
  • 2 tbsp. red wine vinegar
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 c. extra-virgin olive oil

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, toss cucumber, onion, tomatoes, and olives. Gently fold in feta.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk vinegar, lemon juice, and oregano until combined; season with salt and pepper. Slowly stream in oil, whisking until dressing is smooth and emulsified.
  3. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss again to combine.
