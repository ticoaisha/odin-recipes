Cucumber Salad

Creamy fresh cucumber salad that is perfect for the summer time

The recipe for this easy salad came from my grandma, who made it in the summers with fresh dill and cucumbers from her garden.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup reduced-fat sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 3 cucumbers, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced crosswise
  • ½ cup thinly sliced red onion
  • coarse salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix sour cream, lemon juice, and dill together in a bowl
  2. Add cucumbers and onion
  3. Stir to combine
  4. Season with salt and black pepper
